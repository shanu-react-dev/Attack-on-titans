## form in HTML

_Form is a way to collect the data from the users. In HTML form is a tag to create different kind of forms for our websites. Such as Login form, Signup form, Marriage form etc._

_It comes up with multiple input elements to collect the data from the users and several attributes as well to structure the form._

## `action` attribute

_Action attribute is used inside opening form tag it holds URL or path where the data will be sent after submitting the form._
